ABOUT DIB BANK KENYA LTD

DIB Bank Kenya Limited (DIBBKE), is a subsidiary of Dubai Islamic Bank (AE) - a bank that not only gave Shariah compliant banking to the world but continues to keep the flag flying. The bank is built on the age – old values of Progressiveness, Ethics, Innovation and Reliability

Our main aim is to ensure that in all our operations and business dealings, the following are adhered to as part of our brand promise:

  • Open and fair governance
  • Fiscally sound use of investors funds
  • Environmental safeguarding
  • Free Internal Standing Orders
  • Free transaction Fee
  • Professionalism and expertise gained through employee enrichment and development

At DIB, tradition and heritage is blended with a commitment to flexibility, innovation and modernity, so that every customer is provided with comprehensive solutions for any financial need.

MISSION AND VISION

Vision:

To be the most progressive Islamic Financial Institution in the World.

Mission:

To maintain and improve our position as the world’s leading Islamic bank through unsurpassed customer service, innovation, growth and consistent employee engagement.

OUR CORE VALUES – THE DIB WAY

  • Ethics: The bank will consistently project the ethos which permeates the bank, and thus communicate to all stakeholders an image of the ethical Shariah Compliant Bank which the board is striving to create and operate. The bank will also ensure honest and open communication and easy access to information.
  • Innovation: The bank will explore and communicate every opportunity used to identify the needs of its stakeholders and provide effective and innovative ways to service these needs with efficiency, accountability and a caring attitude.
  • Progressiveness: The bank will provide constant updates to all its stakeholders on the goals set and communicate on ideas, creative approaches and suggestions made by stakeholders to ensure continuous improvement in all its operations.
  • Reliability: DIB Bank will ensure accountability and transparency in all its dealings to ensure that the interests of all stakeholders are protected.

OUR PLEDGE TO YOU

DIB Bank pledges to protect all its clients (potential and existing) through ethical, fair and honest treatment at every point of contact. Specifically, we pledge the following:

  1. All dealings with our clients will be open, fair and ethical.
  2. We will treat all our clients with respect and professionalism.
  3. We will evaluate each client’s ability and willingness to repay their facility, to avoid the situation where the client has more debt than he/she can repay.
  4. Our product explanations will clearly include:
    1. Profit rates on facilities;
    2. Fees and charges on any product, and premium on insurance; and
    3. All terms and conditions.
  5. Clients are expected to repay their facilities on time, but if they do not, we will still treat them with dignity. We will never use abusive language or physical force, shout at the client, enter the client’s home uninvited, humiliate the client, or violate the client’s right to privacy as dictated by our privacy policy.
  6. We will inform clients of their right to ask questions and complain, and will educate them about the various ways they can submit questions and complaints to the institution. We will respond to questions and complaints quickly and fairly.
  7. We respect our customers’ privacy and will treat customer information as private and confidential. Clients will receive the institution’s Client Privacy Agreement. Please contact the MacroDreams Customer Service Line if you have questions or compliments
