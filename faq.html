Sultan Current account

An account designed for high net worth customers and is available to both salaried and non-salaried customers. The account is also available in other currencies (i.e USD, GBP, EUR)

Features and Benefits
  • Free E statements.
  • Access to Cheque Book.
  • Access to Sultan Gold Debit Card
  • Free Internal Standing Orders.
  • Free transaction Fee
  • Access to Mobile banking
  • Access to Internet Banking.
  • Access to SMS alerts.
  • Dedicated Relationship Manager
  • Access to Express Counters
  • Access to Financing facilities at preferential rates.
